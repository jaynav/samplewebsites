bikes suited for small errands

well here there are some few choices as well. these bikes although can have come with a bike that is meant for small errands

  1. a tricycle
  2. a trailer adapter
  3. adapter for baskets

adding a basket

well the cheapest alternative for making your errands while not using your car would be to add basket/ basket like accessories to your bicycle. They attach almost anywhere on the bicycle. the only issue with these is that you will have to add several to do a small shopping trip. the other issue with these is that if you add to much weight and you are leaning during a turn, it may cause you to fall due to the weight pushing you to the side. therefore you must have good balance if you are carrying heavy loads in these bags.  the price for these bags are around $30 dollars although there are cheaper ones.

 

 

adding a trailer adapter

trailer adapters are very useful for two reasons, one you can buy an adapter to take you kids with you or you can use it to carry more things when you go shopping. trailers are a convenient way to take your kids with you on short trips. they adapt to almost any bike that is currently available in the market. the current cost of bike trailers cost around $74 dollars to $300 dollars

 

buying a tricycle

well many think that buying a tricycle is something that small children get before they learn to use a bicycle. even thought that is true, they offer a comfortable ride that will allow you to take around town in style while being able to load things in the back of your seat without worrying that you are going to fall over in a turn. these bikes also start out somewhat expensive too. the cheapest bikes are around $249 and they already come with a pretty good size steel basket that can carry about 50Lbs of items. the more expensive tricycles cost (the highest amount is about $710 dollars) the more features you get such as multispeed bikes  and they are lighter too.
