About Us

The CPAC has been a club at the University for over 40 years. Founded in 1963, CPAC has been active in:

  • environmental issues
  • campus green development
  • protection of avian habitats
  • fun with backyard birding
  • helping students enjoy the wonders of nature on campus
  • CPAC is a student led club with a zero carbon footprint.

CPAC is affiliated with the National Audubon Society and Cal Poly Pomona University. Come join us for a club meeting.
